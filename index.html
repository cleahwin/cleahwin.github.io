About Me

I'm currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at the University of Washington, Seattle. I work on projects in the space robotics, LLMs, machine learning, neural engineering, and brain-computer interfaces and have presented my research at international conferences (e.g., ICSE, ICHI, NeurIPS workshop). I am specifically interested in reinforcement learning applications to improve robot learning and the use of LLMs in healthcare.
